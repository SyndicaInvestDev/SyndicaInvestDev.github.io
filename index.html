About

Creating an Angel Syndicate has never been easier

Traditionally, the only people that were able to create a proper angel syndicate (SPVs included) were professional investors, lawyers and tech-wizards. It was especially complicated for people who just wanted to create a vehicle to invest with friends and relatives easily

Tired of not finding enough solutions online, we decided to create Syndica to facilitate the creation of SPVs . Not only do we offer a way to add members to the syndicate, but it also provides a payment gateway and scouting platform to find the best opportunities.
